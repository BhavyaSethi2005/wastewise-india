--- E-WASTE DISPOSAL ---

NATIONAL E-WASTE COLLECTION PROGRAMS:
- All registered e-waste recyclers listed at: cpcb.nic.in (Central Pollution Control Board)
- Producer Responsibility: Under E-Waste Rules 2022, all electronics brands MUST provide take-back
- Samsung: samsung.com/in/takeback — free pickup for old Samsung devices
- Apple: apple.com/in/trade-in — trade-in or free recycling
- Dell: dell.com/en-in/dt/recycling — free drop-off
- HP: hp.com/in-en/recycling — take-back program
- Motorola, Xiaomi: contact customer service — all must by law accept e-waste

CITY E-WASTE COLLECTION CENTRES:
- Indore: IMC (Indore Municipal Corporation) e-waste drive held quarterly. Check imcindore.org
- Delhi: Check dpcc.delhigovernment.gov.in for authorised recyclers
- Mumbai: BMC collection drives. mpcb.gov.in for authorised recyclers
- Bengaluru: BBMP e-waste collection. bbmp.gov.in

--- HAZARDOUS WASTE (BATTERIES, MEDICINES, CHEMICALS) ---

BATTERIES:
- Many electronics stores (Croma, Reliance Digital) have battery collection boxes near exit
- Energizer and Duracell run collection programs at select retailers
- Municipal hazardous waste collection events (check local municipal app or website)

MEDICINES:
- Ask at any government hospital — they have pharmaceutical waste bins
- Many chemist shops in Indore, Mumbai, Bengaluru now accept unused medicines
- Check: pharmacycouncil.org for registered take-back pharmacies

COOKING OIL:
- BIOFUEL INDIA: collectoldcookingoil.com — home pickup for used cooking oil (converts to biodiesel)
- Many municipal bodies in Bengaluru and Chennai run used oil collection drives
- Contact local biodiesel plants

--- TEXTILE WASTE ---

DONATION (good condition clothes):
- Goonj: goonj.org — pan-India network, accepts all clothing
- Local gurudwaras, mosques, temples — usually have collection bins
- iCall India — clothes donation

RECYCLING (unwearable textiles):
- H&M stores in India: in-store textile recycling bins (any brand, any condition)
- Zara India: in-store collection
- Upcyclers like Reweave India: reweave.in

--- BULK / LARGE ITEMS ---

Contact your municipal corporation for bulk item pickup:
- Indore: Call 0731-2972001 (IMC helpline) or use IMC mobile app
- Delhi: MCD helpline: 1800-11-0093
- Mumbai: BMC helpline: 1916
- Bengaluru: BBMP helpline: 080-22975555

--- REPORTING ILLEGAL DUMPING ---
- Swachh Bharat Mission app: Report illegal dumping with photos
- Local ward office
- CPCB complaint portal: cpcb.nic.in/complaints

--- USEFUL APPS FOR WASTE MANAGEMENT (INDIA) ---
- Saahas Zero Waste App: Find nearest recycling drop-off
- Swachh Bharat App: Report waste, track collection
- BBMP App (Bengaluru specific): Schedule pickup
- IMC App (Indore specific): Track waste collection

--- GENERAL RULES EVERY INDIAN SHOULD KNOW ---

1. Under SWM Rules 2016/2026, SEGREGATION AT SOURCE is MANDATORY for all households
2. Bulk generators (>100kg/day) face penalties for non-segregation
3. Burning waste of any kind in open is ILLEGAL under Air Prevention Act
4. Littering in public can attract fines (varies by city: Rs.500 to Rs.5000)
5. E-waste Rules 2022 mandate that all electronics brands MUST take back old products
6. Plastic bags below 75 microns are BANNED nationally since July 2022
7. Single-use plastic cutlery, straws, plates are BANNED nationally since July 2022
